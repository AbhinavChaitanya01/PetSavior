About Us

PetSaviour was a project initiated by Harsh and Abhinav as their exploration project in the University with the aim to help the creatures in need through a synchronised web app, ready to address all their needs.

Our Vision

To make lives of needy and injured animals better and easier

Our Mission

Our mission is to help the speechless creatures lead better life through efforts of the society and enabling individuals to help by making the process hassle-free for them! Also we help individuals find an animal friend which was rescued and needs a better life!

Our Philosophy

We believe in fulfilling our mission by help of NGOs and Individuals who believe in our cause and to deliver the help in an easy manner.

Our Strategy

Our stategy is to engage NGOs,volunteers,individuals and animals to fulfil our mission.
